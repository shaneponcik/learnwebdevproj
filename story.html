Shane's Story:

I started riding in the summer of 2016 and racing in 2017. Over the past two years, I have had the opportunity to meet some great people and learn a lot about myself. I currently am a part of the Strike Cycling Team based out of Texas. I joined the team in 2018 as a category 4 and have since then progressed into a category 2. I have found the support of the team to be crucial in my ability to succeed this year. I look forward to the rest of the season as I continue to learn and improve.
